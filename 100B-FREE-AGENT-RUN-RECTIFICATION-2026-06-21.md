1. The system starts from an original question set.
2. Brown-Hilbert/PID geometry, rule-of-three lanes, 49D+ tuples, project-ID rotators, and port/room emitters generate a unique question variant.
3. C-drive project-name/room identity changing acts as a question identity mutator.

5. 10k rooms and micro-kernel/stubbed dashboard rooms carry tuple-loaded routing state.
6. GNNs, hookwalls, shannon/omnishannon, white rooms, gulps, and super-gulps process returned messages.
